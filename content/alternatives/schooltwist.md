## Common Questions About Switching

**Will I lose my historical data?**  
No. We can help you export data from SchoolTwist and import it into SwiftEnroll, preserving your enrollment history and family records.

**What about my current enrollment period?**  
You can run both systems in parallel during transition, or time your switch between enrollment periods for a clean cutover. Many organizations switch during their off-season for a seamless transition.

**How long does migration take?**  
Most organizations are fully operational on SwiftEnroll within a few days to 2 weeks, depending on your program complexity and data volume.

**Do you offer migration support?**  
Yes! All new customers get onboarding support to help with setup and data migration. We'll guide you through the process and ensure a smooth transition.

**What about the lottery feature?**  
SwiftEnroll's automated lottery is one of our standout features. Unlike SchoolTwist, we include a fair, transparent lottery system that supports ranked-choice preferences, automatically processes enrollments, and manages waitlists—all without manual intervention.
