## Challenges for Arts Program Coordinators

Managing lesson packs, rehearsals, and performance groups requires precision — not spreadsheet gymnastics.

* **Complex scheduling** — overlapping lessons, ensemble rehearsals, and performance calendars that conflict
* **Recurring enrollments** — families want ongoing lessons, not re-registration every term
* **Prerequisite tracking** — ensuring students have the right level or audition status before advanced placement
* **Equipment and materials** — tracking instrument rentals, sheet music purchases, and costume fees
* **Performance logistics** — coordinating who's in which show, managing ticket requests, and family communications
