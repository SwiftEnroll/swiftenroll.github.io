## Challenges for STEM & Academic Programs

Running coding clubs, robotics teams, and tutoring sessions shouldn't require engineering-level spreadsheet skills.

* **Course prerequisites** — ensuring students have the right background before enrolling in advanced topics
* **Session rotations** — managing multi-week modules where students progress through learning sequences
* **Equipment and kits** — tracking which families ordered robotics kits, lab materials, or course supplies
* **Tutoring logistics** — scheduling 1-on-1 or small-group sessions with instructor availability constraints
* **Progress tracking** — coordinating enrollment records with academic performance and completion requirements

## How SwiftEnroll Helps

SwiftEnroll is **enrichment enrollment system** software built for academically-focused programs.

* **Prerequisite enforcement** — set grade-level requirements, prior course completion, or assessment scores
* **Multi-session sequences** — configure learning pathways where completion of Module 1 unlocks Module 2
* **Kit and materials management** — add equipment purchases or rentals to enrollment; track fulfillment
* **Instructor assignment** — match students to tutors or instructors based on availability, expertise, or group size
* **Compliance-ready reporting** — export enrollment and attendance data formatted for grants or district requirements
