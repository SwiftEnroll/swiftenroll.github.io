## Challenges for Early Childhood Programs

Managing preschool enrollments means balancing family needs, licensing requirements, and operational realities.

* **Age cutoffs and readiness** — ensuring children meet birthdate requirements and developmental milestones
* **Waitlist complexity** — tracking priority families, sibling preferences, and rolling admission throughout the year
* **Licensing and compliance** — collecting immunization records, emergency contacts, and required authorizations
* **Multi-session care** — coordinating part-day, full-day, and extended-day programs with different pricing and schedules
* **Family communication** — keeping parents informed about enrollment status, orientation dates, and program updates
