## Challenges for After-School Programs

Running enrichment programs shouldn't mean drowning in spreadsheets and inbox chaos.

* **Manual rosters** that become outdated the moment a family emails a change
* **Parent confusion** about available spots, waitlist status, or schedule conflicts
* **Lottery processes** managed with paper forms and manual randomization
* **Payment tracking** across multiple sessions, families, and partial scholarships
* **Compliance requirements** for enrollment data, approvals, and documentation

## How SwiftEnroll Helps

SwiftEnroll is **after school program software** built for how enrichment coordinators actually work.

* **Real-time availability** — parents see open spots instantly across all sessions and sites
* **Automated lottery enrollment** — fair, transparent selection with audit trails built in
* **Integrated payments** — Stripe processing, payment plans, and scholarship tracking in one place
* **Built-in communication** — automated confirmations, waitlist notifications, and schedule reminders
* **Compliance-ready exports** — enrollment reports formatted for district or grant requirements
