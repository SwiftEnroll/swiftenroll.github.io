## Challenges for Camp Directors

Summer camp registration shouldn't be your full-time job in March and April.

* **Registration surges** that crash email inboxes and overwhelm spreadsheets
* **Waitlist management** across multiple weeks, age groups, and specialty camps
* **Session conflicts** when families want overlapping programs or have scheduling constraints
* **Documentation requirements** — health forms, waivers, emergency contacts collected via scattered PDFs
* **Payment complexity** — deposits, installment plans, sibling discounts, and last-minute cancellations

## How SwiftEnroll Helps

SwiftEnroll is **camp registration software** designed for directors who run real programs, not just collect names.

* **Automated session management** — set capacities, age rules, and prerequisites; the system enforces them
* **Smart waitlist system** — families get instant notifications when spots open; you control priority rules
* **Required documentation** — capture forms, waivers, and uploads at registration; no chase-down emails
* **Flexible payment options** — deposits, payment plans, early-bird pricing, and sibling discounts all configured in minutes
* **Multi-week visibility** — parents see the full summer schedule and register for multiple sessions at once
